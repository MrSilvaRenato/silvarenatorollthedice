Game Description:

Roll the dice is a game of rounds between 2 players, roll the dice to sum randomly points from 1 to 6, the first player to achieve 100 points wins. Is all about luck.

Game Rules:

  • Score winning default is 100 points.
  • Secure your points by pressing the Hold button, obs: You pass your turn to the next player.
  • Double 1, you loose current score and loose your turn. obs: secured points are kept.
  • Double 6, you loose all your points and your turn.
